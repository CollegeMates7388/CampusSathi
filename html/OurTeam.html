CampusSathi

CampusSathi, the Socio-Academic Portal of Axis Colleges, is the most frequently visited website of Axis Colleges. It was started out by a team of three enthusiastic students with an aim to provide a holistic solution to all student needs; those that are encountered during these crucial years of college.In short - We aim to make the life of every Axisian simpler & turn these college years into a smoother ride!.
